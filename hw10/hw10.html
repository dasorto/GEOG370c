Homework 10: In this homework I displayed different maps of Honduras using Geoprocessing tools such as dissolved, buffering, and intersection. The questions I answered in my analysis were: Is there a higher percentage of electricity in homes where wind plants operate? What are the regions with most inequality and what is its relationships with access to electricity?
